About the Game

Merge Food - Chef Decoration combines the excitement of puzzle gameplay with the joy of cooking and decorating. Players merge ingredients to craft unique dishes, unlock rare recipes, and design their dream kitchens. Explore various cuisines, take on culinary challenges, and grow your kitchen empire with the help of a charming cast of characters.
